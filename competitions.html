Reactive Synthesis Competition

The reactive synthesis competition considers two classes of synthesis problems: the synthesis of reactive controllers for a given circuit in AIGER format, and the synthesis of reactive components based on a linear temporal logic specification.

SYNT 2024 will host the Reactive Synthesis competition (SYNTCOMP 2024) whose results will be presented at this workshop.
